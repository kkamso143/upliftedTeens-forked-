Poor mental health in teenagers can significantly impact their overall well-being, academic performance, social relationships, and decision-making abilities. It can lead to difficulties with concentration, memory, and academic success, as well as social isolation and strained relationships with peers and family. Additionally, untreated mental health issues in teens can increase the risk of substance abuse, self-harm, and suicide.

Coping Mechanisms:
- journaling your daily thoughts and feelings
- seeking social support (trusted adult, friends, or family)
- yoga and meditation
- sufficient sleep
- positive affirmations
- setting healthy boundaries
