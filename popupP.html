What Products should I use?

It's important to use healthy products with natural ingredients

Good Products include:

Aleavia Purifying Facial Cleanser:A prebiotic cleanser that balances skin pH and addresses acne-prone, sensitive, dry, and oily skin.

- Kinship Naked Papaya Cleanser: A gentle, milky cleanser that removes dirt and makeup without drying the skin.

- Thrive Natural Care Face Balm: A calming, lightweight moisturizer suitable for all skin types.

- Honest Beauty Gentle Gel Cleanser: A gentle, non-irritating cleanser for normal to combination skin.

- Weleda Skin Food Nourishing Day Cream: A hydrating moisturizer for normal to dry skin.

Bad Skincare Products for Teens:

- Products with harsh chemicals: Avoid products containing phthalates, parabens, PFAS, artificial dyes, formaldehyde, and sodium lauryl sulfate, as they can disrupt hormones and cause skin irritation.

- Exfoliating scrubs: Can be too harsh for teen skin, causing micro-tears and irritation.

- Products with vitamin C: May not be suitable for acne-prone skin and can cause irritation when combined with other acids or retinol.

- Retinol products: Can be irritating, especially for sensitive skin, and increase sun sensitivity.

- Anti-aging products: Not necessary for teens, as they can cause irritation and are often marketed with unrealistic expectations
