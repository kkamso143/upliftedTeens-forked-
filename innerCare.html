Appearance is crucial to us because it plays a huge part in how others see us and how we present ourselves to others, especially as teens who have fluctuating hormones, which causes stress and acne.

Making a habit of using skincare daily can help you destress and improve your skin. But first, you must understand what type of skin you have and the right products to use! You can learn this by contacting your dermatologist or taking this online quiz
